# Balance

Generated cards use costs 1-6 and power 1-12 derived from source attributes, then receive one of twenty deterministic ability templates. Rarity is collection presentation and does not grant hidden competitive rules.

Preset target curve is `1,2,2,3,3,3,4,4,4,5,5,6`. This guarantees early actions while preserving finishers. Validation requires at least one one-cost card and at least six cards costing three or less.

Balance review uses deterministic seeded simulations and records front control, total power, cards stranded in hand, unused orders, initiative changes, banner timing and withdrawal value. Changes to a template must be evaluated across all cards assigned to it, all six presets and representative front categories. Version fields allow future data migrations without changing historical replays.
